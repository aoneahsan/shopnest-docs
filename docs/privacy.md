# Privacy Overview

This page summarizes how ShopNest handles your data. The authoritative, full privacy policy lives in the app at [shopnest.aoneahsan.com/privacy](https://shopnest.aoneahsan.com/privacy); this overview explains the essentials and points you to your controls.

## What ShopNest collects

To run a store, ShopNest collects the data needed to support your account and orders:

- **Account data** — your name, email, and sign-in identity (Google, Apple, or email).
- **Order data** — items purchased, totals, shipping address, and order status.
- **Usage data** — anonymized analytics about how the app is used, to improve it.

## Services ShopNest relies on

ShopNest uses established providers, each for a specific purpose:

- **Firebase / Firestore** — authentication and data storage.
- **Stripe and PayPal** — payment processing. Card details are entered into the provider's secure fields, not stored by ShopNest.
- **FilesHub** — file/image storage (ShopNest does not use Firebase Storage for uploads).
- **Analytics and error monitoring** — anonymized analytics (Amplitude, Firebase Analytics, Microsoft Clarity) and error tracking (Sentry), each active only when configured.

Payment processors act as their own controllers for the payment data you give them; the analytics and error tools receive anonymized signals to help maintain and improve the app.

## Your rights and controls

- **Access and export** your data through your account tools.
- **Delete** your account and associated data via the [data deletion](/guides/data-export-and-deletion) flow.
- **Download invoices** for your records at any time.

These align with data-protection frameworks such as GDPR and CCPA.

## Honest framing

ShopNest collects what it needs to operate a store and uses reputable third parties for payments, storage, and analytics. It does not sell your data, and it shows only first-party promotions rather than third-party ad networks. For the binding details, always refer to the [full privacy policy in the app](https://shopnest.aoneahsan.com/privacy).
