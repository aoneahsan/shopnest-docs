# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using ShopNest. For deeper detail, follow the links into the feature pages.

## General

### What is ShopNest?

ShopNest is a free, modern e-commerce platform where you browse a product catalog, build a cart, check out, and track orders. It also includes accounts, wishlists, a loyalty program, reviews, and an admin dashboard for store owners. See the [introduction](/intro).

### How much does ShopNest cost?

ShopNest is free to use. It shows only first-party promotions for the developer's other apps — there are no third-party ad networks.

### Where can I use ShopNest?

On the web at [shopnest.aoneahsan.com](https://shopnest.aoneahsan.com). Android and iOS builds are in progress.

## Accounts

### Do I need an account to shop?

You can browse, search, compare, and fill a cart without an account. You sign in to check out, track orders, keep a wishlist, and use loyalty.

### How do I sign in?

With Google, Apple, or email. On supported devices you can also enable biometric re-login. See [Accounts & Sign-In](/features/accounts-and-auth).

## Orders and payments

### What payment methods are supported?

Stripe (cards) and PayPal. Card details go into the provider's secure fields, not the storefront. See [Payments & Invoices](/features/payments-and-invoices).

### How do I track my order?

From the track-order page or your dashboard order history. Each order shows its current fulfillment stage. See [Track an order](/guides/track-an-order).

### Can I get a receipt?

Yes. Every order has a downloadable PDF invoice in your order history.

## Privacy

### What data does ShopNest collect?

Account, order, and usage data needed to run the store, as described on the [privacy page](/privacy).

### Can I delete my data?

Yes. ShopNest provides an account/data deletion flow. See [Export or delete your data](/guides/data-export-and-deletion).

## Still need help?

Open the app and use its support/contact options, or reach the developer at [aoneahsan.com](https://aoneahsan.com).
