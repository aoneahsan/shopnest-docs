# ShopNest Quick Start

This quick start takes you from opening ShopNest to placing your first order. The whole flow works in a browser at [shopnest.aoneahsan.com](https://shopnest.aoneahsan.com) — no install required, though you can [install it as an app](/features/pwa-and-notifications) if you prefer.

## 1. Open the storefront

Visit [shopnest.aoneahsan.com](https://shopnest.aoneahsan.com). The home page shows featured products and category entry points. You can browse and add items to your cart without an account; you only need to sign in to check out.

## 2. Find a product

Use any of these paths to reach a product:

1. Pick a **category** from the navigation or home page.
2. Type into **search** to match products by name instantly.
3. Open the **Products** page and apply **filters** (price, category, attributes) to narrow results.

Open a product to see its images, description, price, specifications, and reviews.

## 3. Add to cart

On a product page, choose any options and select **Add to cart**. A cart badge updates immediately. You can keep shopping and adjust quantities later — your cart persists across page reloads and, once you sign in, across devices.

## 4. Create an account or sign in

When you're ready to buy, [create an account](/getting-started/create-your-account) with Google, Apple, or email. Signing in links your cart, saves your addresses, and lets you track orders afterward.

## 5. Check out

Open your cart and start [checkout](/features/cart-and-checkout). The multi-step flow collects your shipping address, then your payment method ([Stripe or PayPal](/features/payments-and-invoices)), then shows a review screen before you confirm.

## 6. Track your order

After you confirm, ShopNest creates an order you can follow on the [track-order page](/features/orders-and-tracking) and in your dashboard. You can download a PDF invoice for any order at any time.
