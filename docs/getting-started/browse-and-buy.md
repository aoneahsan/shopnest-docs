# Browse and Buy on ShopNest

The ShopNest shopping flow moves from discovery to a confirmed order in a few predictable steps: find a product, evaluate it, add it to your cart, and check out. This guide connects those steps so you know where each tool fits.

## Discover products

There are three ways to reach products, and they work together:

- **Categories** organize the catalog into browsable groups from the navigation and home page.
- **Search** matches products by name as you type, so you can jump straight to a known item.
- **Filters** on the Products page narrow a category or search result by price and attributes.

See [Product Catalog](/features/product-catalog) and [Search & Filters](/features/search-and-filters) for the details.

## Evaluate before you buy

ShopNest gives you several ways to judge a product before committing:

1. Open the **product detail page** for images, full description, specifications, and price.
2. Read **reviews** from other shoppers on the same page.
3. Use **quick view** to preview a product without leaving the listing.
4. Add candidates to **compare** and review their attributes side by side.

## Add to cart and adjust

Select **Add to cart** on any product. Open the cart to change quantities, remove items, or apply a coupon code. The cart total updates live, and your cart persists across reloads — and across devices once you're signed in.

## Check out

Start [checkout](/features/cart-and-checkout) from the cart. ShopNest walks you through shipping, then payment ([Stripe or PayPal](/features/payments-and-invoices)), then a review step before you confirm. After confirmation, your order appears in the [tracking](/features/orders-and-tracking) page and dashboard.

## Keep shopping smarter

- Save items you're not ready to buy to your [wishlist](/features/wishlist).
- Earn [loyalty](/features/loyalty) points as you shop and redeem them for rewards.
- Install ShopNest as a [PWA](/features/pwa-and-notifications) for quicker access.
