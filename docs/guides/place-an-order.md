# How to Place an Order on ShopNest

Placing an order on ShopNest means moving products from your cart through a guided checkout to a confirmed purchase. This guide walks the full path so you know what each step expects.

## Before you start

You can browse and fill a cart without an account, but you'll [sign in](/getting-started/create-your-account) to check out. Having a saved address speeds things up, though you can add one during checkout.

## Steps

1. **Add products to your cart.** From any listing, quick view, or product page, select **Add to cart**. Repeat for everything you want.
2. **Open your cart.** Review the items, adjust quantities, and apply a coupon code if you have one. Check the running total.
3. **Start checkout.** Select **Checkout** from the cart.
4. **Enter shipping.** Choose a saved address or add a new one. This is where the order will ship.
5. **Choose payment.** Pick [Stripe (card) or PayPal](/features/payments-and-invoices) and enter your details in the provider's secure fields.
6. **Review.** Confirm the items, totals, shipping address, and payment method on the review step.
7. **Confirm.** Place the order. ShopNest creates it and shows a confirmation.

## After you confirm

Your order is immediately available to [track](/guides/track-an-order), appears in your dashboard's order history, and has a downloadable [PDF invoice](/features/payments-and-invoices).

## Tips

- Save addresses ahead of time so checkout is faster.
- Apply coupon codes in the cart before checkout so the discount is reflected at review.
- Sign in before you start if you want your cart to sync from another device.
