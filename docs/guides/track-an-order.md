# How to Track an Order on ShopNest

Tracking an order on ShopNest shows you where a purchase is in the store's fulfillment process. Once you've [placed an order](/guides/place-an-order), you can follow it on the track-order page or from your dashboard.

## Steps

1. **Sign in** to the account you used to place the order. Orders are tied to your account.
2. **Open your order history** in the dashboard, or go to the **track-order** page.
3. **Select the order** you want to follow.
4. **Read the status.** ShopNest shows the order's current stage — for example placed, processing, shipped, or delivered.
5. **Open the invoice** if you need a receipt; every order has a downloadable [PDF invoice](/features/payments-and-invoices).

## Understanding statuses

A status reflects where your order is in the store's process. It updates as the store advances the order through fulfillment. A status is a clear stage indicator rather than a live courier map, so it answers "what stage is my order at" reliably.

## If something looks wrong

If an order's status seems stuck or incorrect, check that you're signed into the right account, then reach out through the app's support/contact options. Keep your order's invoice handy as a reference.
