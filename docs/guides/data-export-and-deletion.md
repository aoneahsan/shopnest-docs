# Export or Delete Your ShopNest Data

ShopNest gives you control over your personal data: you can request an export of what's stored about you, and you can delete your account and associated data. This guide explains how, and what to expect.

## Your rights

ShopNest's [privacy policy](/privacy) describes the data the app collects and your rights over it, including the right to access, export, and delete. These align with common data-protection expectations such as GDPR and CCPA.

## Delete your account and data

ShopNest provides an in-app **account/data deletion** flow, reachable from the app's data-deletion page. Deleting your account removes your profile and associated data per the privacy policy. Because deletion is permanent, ShopNest confirms before proceeding.

What deletion covers:

- Your **profile** and account record.
- Your **saved addresses** and **wishlist**.
- Account-linked data held for your use of the store.

Order records that a store must retain for legitimate business or legal reasons (for example, transaction history) may be kept as described in the privacy policy, even after account deletion.

## Export your data

To take your data with you, use the account/data tools in your dashboard and the deletion page's export options where available, or request an export through the app's support/contact channel. Your [PDF invoices](/features/payments-and-invoices) are downloadable from your order history at any time as a record of purchases.

## Before you delete

- Download any [invoices](/features/payments-and-invoices) you want to keep.
- Note that deletion is permanent and removes account-linked data.
- Make sure you're signed into the correct account.
