# How to Manage Your ShopNest Account

Your ShopNest dashboard is where you keep your account in order — profile details, shipping addresses, order history, wishlist, and security settings all live there. This guide points you to each.

## Update your profile

Open the **Profile** section of your dashboard to edit your name and contact details. These details appear on your orders and invoices, so keep them current.

## Manage addresses

In **Addresses**, save one or more shipping addresses. Saved addresses appear as options at [checkout](/features/cart-and-checkout), so you don't re-type them each time. Add, edit, or remove addresses as your needs change.

## Review orders and wishlist

- **Orders** shows your full [order history](/features/orders-and-tracking) with statuses and invoices.
- **Wishlist** lists products you've [saved for later](/features/wishlist); move them into your cart when you're ready.

## Security and sign-in

In **Security**, manage your sign-in and, on supported devices, enable or disable [biometric re-login](/features/accounts-and-auth). Biometric login is optional and stored on your device.

## Export or delete your data

If you want to take your data with you or remove your account, follow the [data export and deletion](/guides/data-export-and-deletion) guide. ShopNest gives you control over your data per its [privacy policy](/privacy).
