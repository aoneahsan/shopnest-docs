# Orders and Tracking

An order is the record ShopNest creates when you confirm a purchase. From that point you can follow the order through its stages on a dedicated tracking page, review every past order in your dashboard, and download an invoice for each.

## What happens after checkout

When you confirm at the end of [checkout](/features/cart-and-checkout), ShopNest creates an order containing the items, quantities, prices, totals, shipping address, and payment record. You're taken to a confirmation, and the order is immediately available to track.

## Track an order

The **track-order page** shows an order's current status as it moves through fulfillment stages — for example placed, processing, shipped, and delivered. It gives a clear, at-a-glance answer to "where is my order right now" without digging through emails.

## Order history

Your dashboard keeps a complete **order history**. For each order you can:

- See the items, totals, and current status.
- Open the order's details.
- Download the [PDF invoice](/features/payments-and-invoices).

## Honesty about fulfillment

ShopNest records and displays order status; actual shipping speed and delivery depend on the store's fulfillment. A status reflects where the order is in the store's process — it isn't a courier's live GPS, but a clear stage indicator you can rely on.

## Frequently asked questions

### Where do I track an order?

On the track-order page, and from your order history in the dashboard. Both show the order's current stage.

### Can I get a receipt?

Yes. Every order has a downloadable PDF invoice available from your order history.

### Do I need to be signed in to see my orders?

Yes. Orders are tied to your account, so sign in to view your order history and track active orders.
