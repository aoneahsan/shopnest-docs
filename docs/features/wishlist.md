# Wishlist

The wishlist is where you save products you're interested in but aren't ready to buy. It keeps candidates in one place, tied to your account, so you can come back, compare, and move items into your cart when you decide.

## Saving products

Add a product to your wishlist from its detail page or a listing with the save (heart) action. Saved products appear on your **Wishlist** page in the dashboard, each with its image, name, and price so you can recognize them at a glance.

## From wishlist to cart

A wishlist is a staging area for purchases. From the wishlist you can move an item straight into your [cart](/features/cart-and-checkout) and proceed to checkout, or remove items you no longer want. This makes it easy to collect options over time and buy when you're ready.

## Tied to your account

Because the wishlist is saved to your account, it follows you across devices once you're signed in — save on a phone, review on a laptop. You need an account to keep a wishlist, since it's stored against your profile.

## Frequently asked questions

### Do I need an account for a wishlist?

Yes. The wishlist is saved to your account so it persists and syncs across devices.

### Can I move a wishlist item into my cart?

Yes. Move any saved item into the cart in one step and check out.

### Is the wishlist the same as comparison?

No. The wishlist saves products for later; [comparison](/features/search-and-filters) lines up a few candidates side by side to evaluate them right now.
