# Loyalty Program

The loyalty program rewards you for shopping with ShopNest. You earn points on activity, those points place you in a membership tier, and you redeem points for rewards. It turns repeat shopping into tangible benefits and gives regulars a reason to come back.

## Points

You accumulate **points** through activity in the store. Your current balance is shown on the loyalty dashboard, along with how many points you've earned in total. Points are the currency you spend on rewards.

## Tiers

Members progress through tiers — for example **bronze, silver, gold, and platinum** — based on their standing. The loyalty dashboard shows your current tier and your progress toward the next one, so you can see how close you are to leveling up. Higher tiers represent more engaged shoppers.

## Rewards

Available **rewards** are listed on the loyalty dashboard. When you have enough points, you can **redeem** a reward; if you don't, ShopNest tells you so instead of letting the action fail silently. Redeeming deducts the reward's point cost from your balance and updates your loyalty data.

## Referrals

The program includes a **referral** mechanism with a referral code and a count of successful referrals, so you can bring others to ShopNest as part of your loyalty activity.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I earn points?

Through your shopping activity in ShopNest. Your balance and total earned are shown on the loyalty dashboard.

### What happens if I try to redeem a reward without enough points?

ShopNest tells you that you have insufficient points and doesn't process the redemption, so you always know where you stand.

### How do tiers work?

Tiers (bronze, silver, gold, platinum) reflect your standing, and the dashboard shows your progress toward the next tier.
