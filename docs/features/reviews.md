# Product Reviews

Reviews are shopper feedback attached to products. ShopNest shows ratings and written reviews on product pages so buyers can weigh real opinions alongside the description and specs, and store admins can moderate reviews from the dashboard.

## Reading reviews

On a [product detail page](/features/product-catalog), reviews appear with the product so you can read others' experiences before buying. Ratings give a quick summary signal, while written reviews add the detail behind the score.

## Writing a review

Signed-in shoppers can leave a rating and a written review on products. Reviews are tied to your account, which keeps feedback accountable and lets you manage what you've written.

## Moderation

Store administrators can review and manage submitted reviews from the [admin dashboard](/features/admin-dashboard). This keeps the review surface useful and on-topic for shoppers.

## Honesty about reviews

Reviews reflect individual shopper opinions, not a guarantee of any outcome. ShopNest presents them as feedback to inform your decision; they're one input alongside the product's description, specifications, and price.

## Frequently asked questions

### Can I review a product without an account?

No. Reviews are tied to your account so feedback stays accountable. You can read reviews without signing in.

### Where do reviews appear?

On the product detail page, alongside the product's images, description, and specs.

### Who manages reviews?

Store administrators moderate reviews from the admin dashboard.
