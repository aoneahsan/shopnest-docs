# Blog

The blog is ShopNest's content surface — articles, buying guides, and announcements that help shoppers and bring people to the store. Posts are authored with a rich-text editor in the admin dashboard and read on public blog pages.

## Reading the blog

The blog has a listing of posts and individual post pages. Each post supports rich content — headings, formatting, images, links, and tables — so guides and articles can be as detailed as they need to be. Blog content also strengthens the store's discoverability in search.

## Authoring posts

Store administrators write and edit posts in the [admin dashboard](/features/admin-dashboard) using a rich-text editor. The editor supports the formatting you'd expect for product guides and announcements, including images and structured content, so posts look polished without touching code.

## Why a store has a blog

A blog gives an e-commerce store a place to explain products, compare options, answer common questions, and publish seasonal or promotional content. It complements the [catalog](/features/product-catalog) by adding context that a product page alone can't, and it gives search engines more pages to index.

## Frequently asked questions

### Who can write blog posts?

Store administrators, through the admin dashboard's rich-text editor.

### What kind of content can a post include?

Rich content — headings, formatted text, images, links, and tables — suitable for articles and buying guides.

### Where do shoppers read the blog?

On the public blog listing and individual post pages within the app.
