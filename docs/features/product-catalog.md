# Product Catalog

The product catalog is the heart of the ShopNest storefront: a browsable collection of products organized by category, each with a detail page that carries images, a description, specifications, pricing, and customer reviews. It's where shoppers discover what's available and decide what to buy.

## Categories

Products are grouped into categories that appear in the navigation and on the home page. Selecting a category opens a filtered listing of its products, which you can narrow further with [search and filters](/features/search-and-filters). Categories give shoppers a top-down path into the catalog when they're browsing rather than searching for a specific item.

## Product detail pages

Each product has a dedicated detail page that includes:

- An **image gallery** showing the product from multiple angles.
- A **title, description, and price**, with any applicable discount.
- **Specifications** and attributes relevant to the product.
- **Reviews and ratings** from other shoppers (see [Reviews](/features/reviews)).
- **Add to cart** and **add to wishlist** actions.
- **Related products** and recommendations.

Product images fall back to a built-in placeholder when an image is missing, so listings never show broken images.

## Listings and presentation

Catalog listings present products as cards with image, name, price, and quick actions. From a listing you can open the full detail page, use **quick view** for a fast preview, or add a product to your [comparison](/features/search-and-filters) set. Listings are responsive, working from narrow phone screens up to wide desktops.

## Deals and brands

Beyond standard categories, ShopNest surfaces curated **deals** and **brand** pages so shoppers can browse by promotion or by manufacturer. These complement the category structure for shoppers who think in terms of "what's on sale" or "which brand."

## Frequently asked questions

### How are products organized?

By category, with additional curated views for deals and brands. Search and filters work across the catalog to refine any view.

### Do I need an account to browse the catalog?

No. The entire catalog is browsable without signing in. You only sign in to check out or to save items to a wishlist.
