# ShopNest: A Modern Free E-commerce Platform

ShopNest is a free e-commerce platform where shoppers browse a product catalog, build a cart, complete a guided checkout, and track every order to delivery. It pairs a fast storefront — instant search, faceted filters, and side-by-side product comparison — with the account features people expect from a real online store: sign-in, order history, wishlists, a loyalty program, and product reviews. Store owners get a separate admin dashboard to manage products, orders, customers, content, and analytics.

ShopNest runs on the web at [shopnest.aoneahsan.com](https://shopnest.aoneahsan.com), with Android and iOS builds in progress. It is built with React, TypeScript, Vite, TanStack Router/Query, Radix UI, and Capacitor, on top of Firebase and Firestore. Most of the storefront — search, filtering, comparison, the cart, and PDF invoice generation — runs client-side in your browser.

## Who ShopNest is for

ShopNest serves two audiences from one codebase:

- **Shoppers** get a clean storefront to discover products, compare options, save favorites, check out securely, and follow orders to their door.
- **Store owners and admins** get a dashboard to add and edit products, fulfill and update orders, moderate reviews, publish blog content, and read sales analytics.

## What you can do

- Browse a [product catalog](/features/product-catalog) by category and open detailed product pages.
- [Search and filter](/features/search-and-filters) the catalog, then [compare](/features/search-and-filters) candidates side by side.
- Add items to a persistent [cart and check out](/features/cart-and-checkout) through a shipping → payment → review flow.
- Pay with [Stripe or PayPal and download a PDF invoice](/features/payments-and-invoices).
- [Create an account](/features/accounts-and-auth) with Google, Apple, or email, and re-login with biometrics on supported devices.
- [Track orders](/features/orders-and-tracking), keep a [wishlist](/features/wishlist), and earn [loyalty](/features/loyalty) points.
